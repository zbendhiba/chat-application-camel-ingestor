The "Retirement Money Market" saving account offers individual retirement account holders tax advantages and diversification.

** Fees and Requirements:**
* No monthly maintenance fee
* Minimum deposit to open an account is $100, or a $25 automatic monthly deposit

** Additional Benefits:**
* Automatic investment in a diversified portfolio of stocks and bonds through a third-party provider
* Tax-advantaged retirement savings options, such as Roth IRAs or traditional 401(k)s
* Access to retirement planning tools and resources online
